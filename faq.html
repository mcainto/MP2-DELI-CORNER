  • Can I cancel my order?

    If you need to cancel your order after online check-out, please inform our Customer Service Support at delicorner@gmail.com or call at +63917-210-3354. We will do our best to accommodate your request, however, we cannot guarantee the cancellation if the order fulfillment process had already started. If you cancel after the order has been processed, you may be charged a fee for the perishable items in your order.

  • How will I know if the payment I authorized was successful or not?

    For successful payment, you will receive a response “Success Page (payment authorized)” after the card has been accepted by the payment processor and will be auto redirected to platform. For unsuccessful payment due incorrect card details or with insufficient fund, you will receive a response “Payment Failed (we could not acquire the payment)” and won't be able to proceed.

  • I cannot log in to my account, what should I do?

    Ensure that your email or password was entered correctly and that caps lock isn’t on as passwords are case-sensitive. Use the “eye” icon to make your password visible for an easier introduction. Click “Forgot password” if you still have issues logging in.
